IRIS is a self-contained holographic lens that blurs the boundaries between virtual and real.

Enhancing the features of the classic contact lens, IRIS embeds holographic technology that allows for the overlay of digital images on your everyday life.

IRIS also tracks your biometric data and projects it at any time.

Open up endless possibilities for the interactive manipulation of the world you see.
