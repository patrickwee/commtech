IRIS also tracks your biometric data and projects it at any time.

Education

Helping the leaders of tomorrow.

With IRIS, the world is your classroom. Access millions of educational resources displayed right in front of you, and interact with class material like never before. Schools can use IRIS to have students engage in virtual collaborative activities, or give each student an individualized resource to enhance their learning experience. Furthermore, IRIS’s interactive technology allow for novel ways of teaching difficult or abstract subjects by having students interact virtually with the concepts they are learning.

Travel

See the world through a different lens.

IRIS contains a navigation device that can track your location, show you the shortest routes and traffic congestions. Should you be an avid traveller, IRIS will help you to not only decide what places to visit, but also where to eat and stay. Don’t worry about knowing the language of the foreign country, IRIS will translate everything for you in real-time. It will also provide the information on exchange rates, closest banks and ATMs. Furthermore, IRIS can introduce you to the culture of any country; you will quickly become acquainted with local customs and other considerations like tipping, acceptable gestures, and more.

Sports

Stay on top of your game.

IRIS’s biometric tracking allows you to stay on top of your game, always. Keep track of your heart rate, oxygen levels, calories burnt, and much more information in real-time while you exercise, or save records of your runs or match performance. Moreover, IRIS lets you practice anywhere and anytime, as its retinal projection technology can create a virtual playing field wherever you go.

Medical

Make healthcare great again.

As a device in direct contact with both the outside atmosphere and fluids from the eye, IRIS receives moment-to-moment information about your body’s condition. For instance by tracking the glucose level in tears, diabetes patients can more easily manage their condition. Such information is regularly transmitted to an online medical database, and can then be sent to doctors without need for travel to the hospital. Diagnosis takes place via holo-projected video chat, and medication is either delivered or picked up at the local pharmacy.
